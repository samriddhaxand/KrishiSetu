#  Krishi Setu

## 📌 Overview

**Krishi Setu** is a digital platform designed to bridge the gap between farmers and consumers by enabling direct, transparent, and efficient trade of agricultural products.

It provides a **farmer-to-consumer marketplace** where farmers can list their produce and consumers can purchase fresh goods at fair prices—eliminating the need for middlemen.

---

## 🚀 Features

* 🌾 **Direct Selling Platform**
  Farmers can sell their products directly to consumers.

* 🔍 **Product Transparency**
  Consumers can view details about crops and fertilizers used before placing an order.

* 🤝 **Farmer–Consumer Connection**
  Helps establish a direct and trustworthy relationship between farmers and buyers.

* 🛒 **Wide Variety of Choices**
  Consumers can choose from a wide range of farmers based on their preferences.

* 📦 **Bulk Pre-Order System (Long Buy Option)**
  Consumers can place bulk orders in advance—even during the crop growing stage.
  This ensures early demand for farmers and stable pricing for consumers.
